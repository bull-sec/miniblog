<p style="text-align:center">I see myself through my own eyes.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">But they are no longer my eyes</p>

<p style="text-align:center">This is no longer my body</p>

<p style="text-align:center">These are no longer my legs</p>

<p style="text-align:center">These are no longer my arms</p>

<p style="text-align:center; color:red">I need to scream</p>

<p style="text-align:center">But this is no longer my mouth</p>

<p style="text-align:center">The arms that are no longer mine move</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They have moved like this before</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They will move like this again</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They will move like this forever</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I see this with eyes that are no longer mine</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I remember, with a memory that is no longer my own</p>
