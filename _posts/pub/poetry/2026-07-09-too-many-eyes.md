<br>

<p style="text-align:center">How many eyes is too many eyes?</p>

<p style="text-align:center">However many eyes the thing had.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They blinked, they rolled, they winked.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Some bled, some wept, some blind.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Too many eyes.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Unfocused, wandering, wondering.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Too many eyes.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Focused now, many eyes are one eye.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They see me.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Too many eyes see through me.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">They do not wander.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I dare not move or speak.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">It speaks from a mouth that I cannot see.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">It tells me that it sees me.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I cannot move or speak.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Something silver, something sharp, something quick.</p>

<p style="text-align:center">Now I see, with too many eyes.</p>
