<p style="text-align:center">Flashes of light blind my eyes from up ahead</p>

<p style="text-align:center">The air is dry and thick</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I step, my foot breaks through something</p>

<p style="text-align:center">It crunches and shatters beneath my step</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I do not look down</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I already know what it is beneath my step</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I know who it was beneath my step</p>

<p style="text-align:center">The flashes become a steady stream of light upon me</p>

<p style="text-align:center">I wonder if the next person will know me, beneath their step</p>
