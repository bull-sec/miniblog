### Rat-men Don't Exist

*testing testing one*

*two three four*

The idea of painting a Skaven army always simultaenously fascinated and terrified me, so when I discovered Mordheim and found that I could run a Clan Pesilens warband it tickled the "New Project" part of my brain. It's built around a box of Plague Monks (in all their old-school glory), Skabbiks Plaguepack warband from Warcry, and two adorable little metal rats from the ArcWorld: Duel of the Dead Kickstarter pack.

As builds go I did barely anything in terms of conversion, there are two reasons for that, firstly the sculpts for Skabbiks Plague Pack are fantastic and there isn't much I would actually do to improve them. Secondly, these are the first Skaven minis I've ever built, so I thought I'd ease myself in. That second excuse is a proper cop out, but I'm sticking with it.

The paint job was mostly done using the John Blanche series from Army Painter, as well as some of GW's Contrast paints.
